Call for papers

Guidelines for Abstract:
The Abstract of the paper should be of 250 words stating the background, objectives and brief methodology of work with highlights of the results.

Paper Submission Guidelines:
The abstract may be uploaded online. On acceptance, the full paper may be submitted in the template given in the website (Details will be given in the Second Announcement). After the review by the technical committee, the status will be intimated. The selected papers will be considered for publication in SCOPUS indexed journals.
